Options

  • Good Points Indicators:

    • The GPIs are used in-game to indicate the player that they made a good choice. They're accompanied with sound effects. You can disable the SFX or the GPIs completely in the game settings.

  • Delete Persistent Data:

    To get a new fresh start in the game, you can Delete Persistent Data
    - ALL DATA: This option will wipe all of your progress: CGs, BGM, Achievements, Sprite Sheets, Backgrounds as well as your story progress (your save slots will still be available).
    - STORY BASED: This option will not wipe all of your progress. It'll keep your data such as CGs, BGM, Achievements and many more. It'll instead, only wipe your story based data (endings, routes, ...)
    This option is only available from the main menu.

  • Changing your cursor:

    During a game, you'll get to change your own cursor. New cursors will be unlocked as you progress through the game and try other routes.

  • Naming your save files:

    You can name your save files directly in-game. This option can be disabled in the game menu. To rename your save files, you'll have to click on the save slot and overwrite your save, a confirmation popup will appear to ask you if you want to do so.

  • Deleting your save files:

    You can delete your save files in-game so that when you don't need the said save file, you can delete it.

WARNING: Deleting any data from the game is an action that cannot be undone. Confirmation popup will appear but be careful with your deletions.
