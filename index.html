Press the keys : "Shift + A" to access to the accessibility menu: The menu has a self voicing feature and the option to switch to more accessible fonts such as Open Dyslexic or the default font of Ren'Py.

Put the game in fullscreen by pressing the "F" key.

To return to the previous choice / dialogue, press the "Back" button or scroll back with your mouse.

To return to the game menu to save or to change the settings, press "Escape" or the right click of your mouse.

To hide the UI of the game, you can press the "H" key or the middle click of your mouse (clicking on your scroll wheel)

To take a screenshot of the game, press the "S" key WARNING FOR MAC USERS: This will make the game crash, please use the normal screenshot shortcut (Command + Shift + 3)

The "History" menu will log every dialogue you've seen so far.

To skip, press the "CTRL" key. You can also check the "Unseen Text" category in the settings.
